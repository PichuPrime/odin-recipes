Clam Chowder

Description

This New England clam chowder recipe is rich, creamy, and full of coastal flavor.

Ingredients

  • Bacon
  • Vegetables: Chopped Onion and cubed potatoes
  • Water: One and a half cups of water
  • Seasonings: Salt and Pepper
  • Half-and-half and butter
  • Clams: Canned minced clams

Steps

  1. Cook the bacon and onion.
  2. Stir in the potatoes and water, then season.
  3. Boil, uncovered, then simmer until potatoes are tender.
  4. Stirn in half-and-half, butter, drained clams, and half of the clam liquid.
  5. Cook until heated through.
